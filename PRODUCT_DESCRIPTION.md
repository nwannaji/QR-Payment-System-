# QR Pay — The Instant Payment Solution for Nigerian Businesses

## What Is QR Pay?

QR Pay is a mobile payment app that lets your customers pay you by simply scanning a QR code with their phone. No POS terminal needed. No cash to count. No waiting.

You generate a QR code. Your customer scans it, enters the amount and their PIN, and the money lands in your wallet instantly. That's it.

**One scan. One tap. Money received.**

---

## The Problem We Solve

Every day, over 40 million small businesses across Nigeria lose time, money, and customers to payment friction:

- **POS terminals are expensive** — rental fees, network charges, and maintenance add up
- **Cash handling is risky** — errors, theft, and the time spent counting and making change
- **Transfers take too long** — account numbers, bank apps, waiting for confirmations
- **Network is unreliable** — a bad connection means a lost sale

QR Pay removes every one of these barriers. If you have a phone, you can accept payments.

## How It Works

```
You display your QR code  →  Customer scans it  →  Enters amount + PIN  →  Done. Money received.
```

**For the customer:**

## Why QR Pay Is Different

### Payments That Feel Instant

When you tap "Pay," the payment confirms on your screen in under a second. Not 3 seconds. Not 5 seconds. Under one second. You see success immediately and go about your day.

How? The app is smart enough to show you the result the instant you confirm, while the server processes in the background. If anything goes wrong on the server side, you're notified right away and your balance is restored. But in the vast majority of cases, it just works.

### Works When the Network Doesn't

Nigerian networks can be unpredictable. QR Pay is built for that reality.

- **No signal? No problem.** Your data still loads from your phone's local cache. Your balance, your history, your QR code — all visible even when completely offline.
- **Payment failed because of a bad connection?** The app saves your payment attempt and lets you retry safely when you're back online. And because every payment carries a unique safety key, you will never be charged twice — even if you retry.
- **Auto-retry for top-ups.** If a wallet top-up fails because of the network, it automatically retries when your connection is restored. You don't have to do anything.

### Never Double-Charged

This is the detail that matters most: if a payment request times out on your phone but the server actually received and processed it, retrying will not charge you again. Every payment carries a unique identifier that the server recognizes — so a retry simply returns the original result, not a new charge. Your money is protected by design, not by luck.

### Your Data Loads Instantly

Open the app. Your balance is already there. Switch tabs. Your history is already there. No loading spinners. No blank screens. QR Pay shows you what it already knows the moment you look, then quietly refreshes with the latest data in the background.

### Scanning Is Instant Too

When you scan a merchant's QR code, the payment screen opens immediately — before the server even verifies the code. If there's a problem (expired code, wrong merchant), the app catches it and tells you. But most of the time, it's just scan and go.

## Security — Your Money Is Safe

### Your PIN Never Leaves Your Phone
When you enter your 4-digit PIN, it's scrambled into an unreadable code before it ever travels over the network. Even if someone intercepted the data, they could not read your PIN.

### Your Session Is Protected
Your login tokens refresh automatically in the background. If your session expires, the app handles it silently — you stay logged in without interruption.

### Bank-Level Encryption
All communication between your phone and the server is encrypted. In production, the app enforces HTTPS and certificate pinning to prevent anyone from impersonating the server.

### Wrong Charge? Reversed Automatically
If the server rejects a payment after you've seen the success screen (insufficient balance, wrong PIN, etc.), the app immediately reverses the charge on your end and tells you what happened. Your balance is always accurate.

## Questions People Ask

**Do I need a POS terminal?**
No. Your phone is your POS. Display the QR code on your screen or print it.

**What if the network is bad?**
QR Pay works even with no signal. Your data is cached on your phone, and failed operations auto-retry when you're back online.

**Can I be charged twice if I retry a payment?**
No. Every payment carries a unique safety key. If you retry a payment that the server already processed, you get the original result back — never a duplicate charge.

**How do I get my money out?**
Link your Nigerian bank account in the app. Withdraw from your wallet balance anytime.

**Is my PIN safe?**
Yes. Your PIN is converted into an unreadable code before it ever leaves your phone. It is never stored or sent in plain text.

**What happens if a payment fails?**
If the server rejects it, the charge is reversed immediately and your balance is restored. If it failed because of the network, you can safely retry — the unique payment key ensures no double charge.

**Which banks are supported?**
All major Nigerian banks — Access Bank, GTBank, Zenith Bank, UBA, First Bank, Fidelity Bank, Kuda, Opay, Palmpay, and more.

**How fast is a payment?**
Under a second from the customer's perspective. The success confirmation appears instantly, and the server processes in the background.
